About the creator

I am Harsh Ashokbhai Patel, a Computer Programming Diploma student at Seneca College. For my Web222 course, I've chosen to create a website dedicated to the globally popular game, Monopoly. This website aims to tap into a broad audience, offering a range of engaging content that explores the game's strategies, historical context, and educational value. It also aspires to unite Monopoly enthusiasts worldwide, fostering a community for shared experiences and organized events. By providing game updates and selling related merchandise, the site aims to become a valuable resource and potential revenue stream, effectively merging my passion for Monopoly with my web development skills.
